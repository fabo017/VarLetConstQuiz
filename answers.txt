What is the difference between var and let?

    with 'var' you can reassign and redeclare. with 'let' you cannot redeclare. 'let' is block scoped

What is the difference between var and const?

    with 'var' you can reassign and redeclare. with 'const' you cannot reassign or redeclare. 'const' is block scoped

What is the difference between let and const?

    with 'let' you can reassign but not redeclare. with 'const' you cannot reassign or redeclare
